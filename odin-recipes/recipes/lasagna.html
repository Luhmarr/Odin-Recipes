Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

Cooking

The dish should take about 5 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Leave the foil on for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before cutting into it. Enjoy!
